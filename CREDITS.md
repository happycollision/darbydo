- [chiptune3](https://github.com/DrSnuggles/chiptune) - ES6 module with AudioWorklet for playing tracker music
- [libopenmpt](https://lib.openmpt.org/libopenmpt/) - Cross-platform C++ and C library to decode tracked music files
